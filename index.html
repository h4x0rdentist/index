Anish has extensive experience treating nervous patients returning to the dentist after many years of not going. He is gentle, a good listener to his patients and empathetic. He prides himself in providing high quality dental care and is committed to openness and transparency with his patients.

Anish graduated from King's College London Dental Institute in 2010, followed by two years of foundation training in General Dental Practice and in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery departments in Luton, Bedford and Milton Keynes Hospitals.

After some years working in general dentistry, Anish spent one year as a research student in Dental Public Health at Tokyo Medical and Dental University, Japan before returning to dentistry in Essex. Anish provides all aspects of general dentistry including restorative dentistry and dental implants.

In his spare time he grows chilli peppers and is a fan of the Home Assistant open source project.
